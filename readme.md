# My Story

For more than **6 years**, I actively contributed to open source under my previous GitHub account:

### https://github.com/gauravmandall

That account contained

- **350+ public repositories**
- **3M+ lines of code**
- Thousands of commits
- Years of experiments, learning, and open-source work

Unfortunately, the account was suspended by GitHub.

The most difficult part wasn't losing the repositories—it was losing six years of work **without receiving any suspension notification or warning email**.

While I continue working with GitHub Support regarding the account, I decided not to stop building.

**This account is my fresh start.**

Every new repository here represents the next chapter.
